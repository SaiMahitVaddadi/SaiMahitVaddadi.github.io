Approach

Agents that know their limits.

The most dangerous AI agent is one that confidently does the wrong thing. Every agent I build has explicit scope boundaries, human escalation paths, and audit trails — not as afterthoughts, but as first-class design constraints.

That is the OOD detection mentality applied to agentic systems: know when you are outside the distribution, and hand off accordingly.
